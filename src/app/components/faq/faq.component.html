Frequently Asked Questions & Notes

What do the Trigger Types mean?
Type Explanation
Full-Auto Fires continuously while button is held down.
Held Fires continuous beam while button is held down.
Semi-Auto Fires once per click.
Duplex Fires once on click and once on release.
Burst Fires a number of times per click.
Charge Charges up when fire is held down, fires on release.
Spool-Up Fires continuously while button is held down, fire rate increases over a short period.
Detonate Detonates active projectiles.
Full Charge Charges up when fire is held down, only fires on release when fully charged.
Force Charge Weapon automatically charges on click and fires when fully charged.
Full-Auto Burst Fires bursts continuously while button is held down.
Throw Weapon automatically charges on click and is thrown when fully charged, losing access to it until picked up or when the weapon automatically returns after a set period of time.
Mag Burst Fires a number of times equal to the current maximum magazine size on click.
